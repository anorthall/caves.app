Using spreadsheets to maintain a record of caving activity is cumbersome and inconvenient — caves.app solves this problem.

caves.app is easily used on any device with an internet connection, and has a minimalist interface that is designed to make recording data quick and easy. Your privacy and the protection of your data is my priority — all of your information is visible only to you (unless you opt to make it public), and other users cannot (by default) see what caves you have added.

Your data is your property. If you do not find the website useful then you can export your data to a CSV file at any time. I take being entrusted with your (potentially irreplaceable) data very seriously and best efforts are made, including regular off-site backups, to protect against any loss of recorded data.

For frequently asked questions, as well as contact details for the administrator, please visit the help page.
