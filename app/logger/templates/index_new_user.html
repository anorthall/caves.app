I thought I'd give you a few hints on how to best use the site. This page will disappear once you've added three trips. Follow the steps below to get started:

  1. Head across to your user profile and add some details about your location, and what caving clubs you are a member of. Please note that timezone handling and distance unit conversion is not yet implemented. All timezones are in UTC (GMT+0000) and all distances are in metres.
  2. Add your first trips! The form should be straightforward enough and the only required data to create a trip is the cave name and trip start time.
  3. Enjoy the website! It is being actively developed and you may come across features that do not work properly. If you do, please let me know immediately by sending an email to admin@caves.app and I will do my best to fix them.
