About caves.app

Using spreadsheets to maintain a record of caving activity is cumbersome and inconvenient. caves.app solves this problem. Initially developed for my own personal use, I have made the service available to all in the hope other cavers will find it useful.

caves.app is easily used on any device with an internet connection, and has a minimalist interface that is designed to make storing your data quick and easy. This website is not supposed to be a database of caves - all of your added data is completely private (unless you opt to make it public), and other users cannot (by default) see what caves you have added.

Your data is completely secure on caves.app. If you do not find the website useful then you can export your data to a CSV file at any time. I take being entrusted with your (potentially irreplaceable) data very seriously and hourly backups of all user data are performed.

Current and planned features

This website is in active development and not all features are currently implemented. Notably, distance unit handling is not implemented. All units of distance are measured in metres. Public profiles are also currently not implemented. Even though you may select a privacy setting for your trips and profile, it has no effect, as there is no public view for trips or profiles yet.

Other features which will be implemented but are not yet include: the adding of photos to trips, a trash bin for deleted trips (all deletions are permanent!), a friends/social 'timeline' functionality, and a 'trip report' functionality which would show rich text trip reports in the style of a blog post.
