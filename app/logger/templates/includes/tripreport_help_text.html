Trip reports are an optional add-on to a trip, used to store a comprehensive account of what happened, using a full range of rich-text formatting options.

Trip reports that are public will be available online for anyone to read. If you want your trip report to be private, or limited to your friends, simply select the appropriate privacy option. The public URL of a trip report will be visible on the trip page.
