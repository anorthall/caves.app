Welcome!

caves.app is the modern way to log your underground activity

A streamlined, mobile-friendly interface allows you to record and view your caving trips from anywhere. Keep detailed records of everything you've done — who was there, the distance you surveyed, how long you spent underground, and more.

You can easily share your activities with friends via your public profile, with the option to keep your trips private if you prefer.

The service is free to use and you have complete control over your data. There is no reason not to give it a go! Want more information? Have a look at the about page.
